About Us

Bhardwaj Dairy was established in 1985 by Mr. Ramesh Bhardwaj with a small herd of cows and a passion for providing pure, fresh dairy products to the local community. What started as a small family business has now grown into a trusted name in dairy products, serving thousands of families across the region.

For over three decades, we have maintained our commitment to quality, purity, and traditional methods of dairy production. Our cows are fed organic feed and are well-cared for, ensuring the highest quality milk and dairy products.
